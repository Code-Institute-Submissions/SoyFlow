WHAT IS DOMINICAN RAP?

The Dominican Rap (Latin hip hop) is a musical genre and a cultural movement that covers various styles of rap music as well as different artistic manifestations such as graffiti or breakdance made in Latin America and Spain. Rap has become the new Latin American protest song, especially among those grown in the 80s, in a context of inflationary processes, structural adjustments and the consequence of the implementation of neoliberal policies recommended by international organizations.

In the Dominican Republic they stand out as the popular Conscious Pencil, Joa, Beethoven Villaman, Arianna Puello, Dkano, Melymel, Shelow Shaq, Monkey Black, Mr. KR, Street Poet, Villain Sam, Black Point, Henry G, Mozart La Para, Secret The Baby Bottle, Sensato Del Patio, Gnomic, Zawezo Del Patio, Nipo, Nukleo Solomon, Blick Brow and El Fother. Also stand out groups or alliances of rappers such as El Batallón (Big O, Big-K, Nuevotono, Caja Blanca, Night Surgeon, TYS and Alex B) and the "Lo correct" business cooperative (UFO, Basic, SinFin, Facundo González ), Stros the Psychologist, Black Circuit, Upper Room and the Above Family The 90s Sheng, Black Hp, Mafuul Flay, Path 04 (Mr Minister, Concept, Fubu), Maximum Legitimate, HST, Mother Earth Metaforus, Saito Kastro, Hardcore En His Radio, Zodiac RDK, 809, Dapely, Gaudy Mercy, Anonymous, Supreme Radical, Leviatan, Dj OnSIX, DJ Avana, Dj Strike One, Dj Scuff, Scratch Master Jesus, DJ Mute, Dj Stan, Dj Rapha, Dj Alfa, Mutant Fingers, Meka Alliance, The Coalition, Nino Freestyle Revolutionary Camp, among others.
